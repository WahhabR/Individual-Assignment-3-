John James Audubon

John James Audubon was a prolific artist and painter born on April 26, 1785 in Haiti. He was fascinated with wildlife and made it his life goal to paint every bird. He would compile his work into a book titled The birds of America. Every bird that is showcased in this gallery, has been observed and drawn by Audubon. Through his love of nature and birds, many new species were also discovered by him and his work continues to live on.
