A Sanctuary of Birds

Flamingo

Flamingos are found in tropical and subtropical areas.

Blue Jay

The blue jay is native to eastern North America. It lives in most of the eastern and central United States.

Canary

The canary is native to the Canary, Azores, and Madeira islands.

Parrot

The Parrot is native to tropical and subtropical regions around the world.

Hummingbird

Hummingbirds can be found from Alaska to Tierra del Fuego.
