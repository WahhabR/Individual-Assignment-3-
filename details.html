Flamingo

Flamingos are known for their characteristic tall and pinkish appearance. Flamingos are not only pink however, they can also be pale, orange or red. They are found in warmer regions likes lagoons or lakes. Flamingos eat small organisms from water where they use their beaks to filter shrimp and plankton. They are known to live in large colonies. Flamingos are terrific flyers and are able to travel as fast as 40 mph.
